The Nairobi Railway Museum is a renowned historical center in Nairobi, Kenya, contiguous to the Nairobi railway station. Holding shows from the old East African Railways, it was opened in 1971 by East African Railway and Harbours Corporation.

The museum gives great info and background on Kenya up until the present day, telling the story through artifacts, pictures, maps, and an AMAZING guide. The museum also features demonstrations, presentations and voiceovers to illustrate the evolution and mechanism of working of the various railway engines and sophisticated locomotives.

Railway Museum Address: Uhuru rd, Nairobi, Kenya
